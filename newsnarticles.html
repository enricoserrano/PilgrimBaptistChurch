Church Organization

On the 13th of July 2014, Pilgrim Baptist Church was fully organized into a local, visible, independent, New Testament Baptist Church. Rev. Gerry Nable, Head pastor of Bethany Baptist Church, Makati, Philippines officiated the ceremony at Howick Leisure Centre through the authority of Calvary Baptist Church Philippines. It was also witnessed by 6 Fundamental Baptist Churches in Auckland who joyfully celebrated the start of a local visible independent Baptist Church in Auckland, New Zealand.
